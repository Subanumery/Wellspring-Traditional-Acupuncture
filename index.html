About Emma

My love of acupuncture originally stemmed from the personal benefit I received. My passion is fuelled by the wonderful people I continue to treat. It is a beautiful, safe system of medicine that everyone should try.

Emma graduated in 2007 from the College of Traditional Acupuncture (accredited by Oxford Brookes University), where she was awarded a Licentiate and BA (Hons) (1st Class) in Traditional Acupuncture.

As a member of the British Acupuncture Council (MBAcC), the UK's largest regulatory body for practitioners of traditional acupuncture, Emma adheres to strict Codes of Safe Practice and Professional Conduct and complies with current Health and Safety legislation. Continuing Professional Development is mandatory.

She is a member of a voluntary register that has been accredited by the Professional Standards Authority. The Accredited Voluntary Register was established by the Government to improve standards and safety for the benefit of the public.

Emma is fully insured to practise and is registered with East Devon District Council.

She is also a Reiki II Practitioner.
